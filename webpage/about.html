About Vectra Automation, Inc.

Vectra Automation provides Robotic Process Automation (RPA) for 3D CAD Engineering Design.

Our flagship Auto2D RPA platform completely automates the generation of Engineering Drawings for Mechanical Systems and Components. As a powerful add-on to leading 3D CAD software platforms, Auto2D RPA leverages cloud-based proprietary algorithmic processing to transform 3D models to 2D Engineering Drawings (or Annotated 3D models) in a matter of hours, not weeks.

By automating the tedious, time-consuming, error-prone work of manual 2D attributing, Design Teams can dramatically reduce costs, free-up thousands of project hours for design flexibility, and eliminate the need for outsourcing and wasteful, redundant QC - and proprietary 3D content never leaves the designer workstation. Auto2D RPA can be configured to any OEM Design Standard for Automotive, Aerospace, Defense, and other Heavy Industry applications for Product and Manufacturing Engineering Design.
