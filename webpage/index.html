Zoe: A Covid-19 Resource Tracker

Zoe is our resouce bot, trained to help you. The word "Zoe" means life in Greek.
We hope to assist you in this battle against the Coronavirus.

Zoe can fetch and display sources to find resources such as oxygen, hospital beds, etc. from posts on Twitter.
Zoe can also give you relelvant information about the vaccination drive in India.
